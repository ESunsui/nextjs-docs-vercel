# Tracking Mode Switch

![makifbt_trackersw_1](image/guide_makifbt/8.jpg#pic_center)

在MakiFBT中，我们提供两种不同的虚拟追踪器生成方案：3 Tracker模式与5 Tracker模式。

In MakiFBT, we provide two different virtual tracker generation schemes: 3 Tracker mode and 5 Tracker mode.

3Tracker模式对应：腰部，左脚，右脚。与头显、手柄合成6点追踪。

3Tracker mode corresponds to: waist, left foot, right foot. Synthesize 6-point tracking with the headset and handle.

![makifbt_trackersw_2](image/guide_makifbt/8-2.jpg#pic_center)

![makifbt_trackersw_3](image/guide_makifbt/10.jpg#pic_center)

5Tracker模式对应：腰部，左脚，右脚，左膝与右膝。与头显、手柄合成8点追踪。

5Tracker mode corresponds to: waist, left foot, right foot, left knee and right knee. Synthesize 8-point tracking with the headset and handle.
