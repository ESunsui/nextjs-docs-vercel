# Toggle Display Item

这一些选项能够显示/隐藏特定的项目。

These options can show/hide specific items.

![makifbt_disp_1](image/guide_makifbt/6.jpg#pic_center)

## 骨骼 Skeleton

![makifbt_disp_2](image/guide_makifbt/13-1.jpg#pic_center)

用一系列绿色线段展示的人体关键点重建模型

Reconstruction model of the human body key points shown by a series of green lines

## VR设备 VR Devices

![makifbt_disp_3](image/guide_makifbt/13-2.jpg#pic_center)

您实际使用的VR设备（我们假设您只有最基础的头显与一对控制器）与我们生成的VMT追踪器

Your actual VR device (we assume you only have the most basic headset and a pair of controllers) and the VMT tracker we generated

白色立方体：HMD

White Cube: HMD

红色/蓝色：左手侧/右手侧

Red/Blue: left hand side/right hand side

圆柱体：手柄

Cylinder: controller

VIVE Tracker样式：生成的VMT追踪器

VIVE Tracker Style: Generated VMT Tracker

腰部追踪器以杂色表示

Waist tracker is represented by mixed color

## 坐标系 Coordinate

![makifbt_disp_4](image/guide_makifbt/13-3.jpg#pic_center)

展示系统所使用的坐标系轴向。

Displays the axis of the coordinate system used by the system.

红色：x轴，前后

Red: x-axis, front and back

绿色：y轴，上下

Green: y-axis, up and down

蓝色：z轴，左右

Blue: z-axis, left and right
