# Parameter Modification

In MakiFBT, we define a series of parameters to complete the mapping relationship between the real space and the game world. Only reasonable parameters can enable you to obtain correct motion capture results.

In MakiFBT, this series of parameters can be automatically generated according to your state, and this series of parameters is correct most of the time.

However, if there is a problem with the parameters, or if you want to do some fine-tuning, you can directly modify the most important parameters.

To manually override a parameter, check the Enable Parameter Modification option box.

<center>![makifbt_param_1](image/guide_makifbt/5.jpg)</center>

## Plane position parameter

The plane position parameter is used to adjust your front, back, left, and right positions in the space. You can adjust the Left/Right, Front/Back two parameters.

These two parameters can be adjusted when you find that your location differs from where the tracker was generated.

<center>![makifbt_param_2](image/guide_makifbt/15-1.jpg)</center>

## Rotation parameter

The rotation parameter is used to adjust your rotation in space around the world origin.

When you find that your movement direction is inconsistent with the Tracker's movement direction, you can first adjust the rotation parameter to align the rotation, and then adjust the position parameter to align the position.

<center>![makifbt_param_3](image/guide_makifbt/15-2.jpg)</center>

## Scaling Parameter

The scale parameter is used to adjust your size in space.

When you find that your range of motion is inconsistent with that of the Tracker, adjust the scaling parameter.

<center>![makifbt_param_4](image/guide_makifbt/15-3.jpg)</center>
