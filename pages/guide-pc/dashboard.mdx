# SteamVR Interface

你可以直接在SteamVR的Dashboard中与MakiFBT交互！VR端与PC端的操控与功能完全一致，您无需脱下VR头显即可实时的调整各项设置。

You can interact with MakiFBT directly in the SteamVR Dashboard! The controls and functions of the VR side and the PC side are exactly the same, and you can adjust various settings in real time without taking off the VR headset.
